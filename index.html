Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shiba Inu (SHIB) cryptocurrency?

Shiba Inu (SHIB) is a decentralized cryptocurrency created in August 2020 as an Ethereum-based alternative to Dogecoin. It's nicknamed the 'Dogecoin killer' and features a Shiba Inu dog as its mascot. SHIB is an ERC-20 token that operates on the Ethereum blockchain.

What factors influence SHIB's price?

SHIB's price is influenced by several factors including overall market sentiment, Bitcoin's price movement, development progress within the Shiba Inu ecosystem, token burns, social media attention (especially from influential figures), exchange listings, and broader economic trends affecting the cryptocurrency market.

What is the relationship between SHIB and other tokens like LEASH and BONE?

SHIB, LEASH, and BONE are all part of the Shiba Inu ecosystem. While SHIB is the primary token, LEASH (originally created as a rebase token) and BONE (governance token) serve different purposes within the ecosystem. BONE allows holders to vote on proposals in the community's governance system, while LEASH has a significantly lower supply and different tokenomics.
