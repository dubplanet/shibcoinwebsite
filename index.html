What is Shiba Inu (SHIB) cryptocurrency?

Shiba Inu (SHIB) is a decentralized cryptocurrency created in August 2020 as an Ethereum-based alternative to Dogecoin. It's nicknamed the "Dogecoin killer" and features a Shiba Inu dog as its mascot.

How accurate is the SHIB price data on ShibaInuCoinTracker.com?

ShibaInuCoinTracker.com provides real-time price data directly from the CoinGecko API, which aggregates prices across major cryptocurrency exchanges. Our data is updated every minute to ensure accuracy.

How can I buy Shiba Inu (SHIB)?

You can purchase SHIB on popular cryptocurrency exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, and KuCoin. You'll need to create an account, verify your identity, deposit funds, and then place an order to buy SHIB.

What factors affect the SHIB price?

SHIB price is influenced by several factors including overall market sentiment, Bitcoin price movements, adoption news, project developments, celebrity endorsements (particularly Elon Musk tweets), token burns, and macroeconomic factors.
