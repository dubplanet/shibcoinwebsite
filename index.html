Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shiba Inu (SHIB) cryptocurrency?

Shiba Inu (SHIB) is a decentralized cryptocurrency created in August 2020 by an anonymous person or group known as "Ryoshi." It's an ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain that began as an experiment in decentralized community building and was dubbed the "Dogecoin killer."

Who created Shiba Inu coin?

Shiba Inu was created by an anonymous individual or group using the pseudonym "Ryoshi." Unlike many crypto projects, Ryoshi stepped away from the project in May 2021, stating that true decentralization requires the founders to step back.

What is the total supply of SHIB tokens?

SHIB was initially launched with a total supply of 1 quadrillion tokens. However, approximately 50% of the supply was sent to Vitalik Buterin's wallet, who burned 90% of his holdings (410 trillion SHIB) and donated the remaining 10% to the India Covid-Crypto Relief Fund.

What is the Shiba Inu ecosystem?

The Shiba Inu ecosystem consists of three main tokens: SHIB (the main currency), LEASH (originally a rebase token, now with limited supply), and BONE (governance token). The ecosystem also includes ShibaSwap DEX, Shibarium (Layer-2 solution), NFT projects, and metaverse development.

When did Shiba Inu gain popularity?

While created in 2020, SHIB exploded in popularity during early 2021 following Dogecoin's rise and Elon Musk's crypto tweets. It saw major price surges in May and October 2021, with the latter pushing it briefly into the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market cap.

What is ShibaSwap?

ShibaSwap is Shiba Inu's decentralized exchange (DEX) launched in July 2021. It allows users to trade cryptocurrencies, stake tokens (called "BURY"), provide liquidity ("DIG"), and earn rewards. It forms the core of SHIB's DeFi capabilities.

What is Shibarium?

Shibarium is Shiba Inu's Layer-2 blockchain solution built on top of Ethereum, launched in 2023. It aims to provide faster transactions with lower fees while maintaining security. It enables more scalable development of applications within the Shiba ecosystem.

What makes Shiba Inu different from Dogecoin?

While both are dog-themed cryptocurrencies, Shiba Inu is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum, whereas Dogecoin has its own blockchain. SHIB has a comprehensive ecosystem with multiple tokens and DeFi applications, while Dogecoin functions primarily as a currency. SHIB also has a much higher total supply.

Has Shiba Inu been involved in any charitable work?

Yes. Most notably, when Vitalik Buterin donated about $1 billion worth of SHIB tokens to India's COVID-19 Relief Fund in 2021. The community has also supported various charitable initiatives through the years.

What is the "SHIB Army"?

The "SHIB Army" refers to the dedicated community of Shiba Inu supporters and investors. Known for its passionate engagement on social media, the community has been instrumental in SHIB's growth, marketing, and adoption without traditional corporate backing.
